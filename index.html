Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage your construction projects effectively

Main Dashboard

Get a clear, real-time view of what's happening on site—all in one place. Instantly see today's manpower, active tasks, subcontractors, and submitted reports. Know exactly where your project stands, spot delays before they happen, and make data-backed decisions with confidence.

Smart Planning

Plan ahead, outsource smarter, and stay in control. With just one click, send RFQs to your vendor list—or open it to the wider market—and watch the best prices come to you. Know what matters most right now, stay ahead of deadlines, and lead your team like a boss. Impress your company with proactive planning and visible results.

Real-time Monitoring

Track project progress, manpower, and resources in real-time. Get instant notifications about important updates and potential issues.

Task Management

Organize and track construction tasks, assign responsibilities, and monitor completion status with our intuitive interface.

Subcontractor Management

Efficiently manage subcontractors, track their work, and maintain clear communication channels for better project coordination.

Vendor Directory

Effortlessly organize your vendors with a fully customizable directory. Create tailored lists that match your workflow, add or remove companies as needed, and search for new vendors based on your unique requirements. Access detailed profiles, review their business activities, and rate them for future projects. Plus, send direct messages to inquire—all from one centralized hub, always ready for your next project.

Quotation Management

Manage all your project quotations in one central place. Upload your material list to automatically send out requests to the right companies—or manually select your own list. Get competitive pricing fast, communicate directly, and even send contracts—all seamlessly integrated for a hassle-free experience.

Execution

Stay fully informed with real-time daily updates sent straight from your vendors' mobile devices. Monitor overall progress effortlessly—from big milestones down to minute details—with alerts if anything goes wrong. Export individual vendor reports, complete with pictures and verified proof, so you can discuss issues accurately in meetings without relying on unverified claims.
